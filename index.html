Abstraktius Artimus: Artist Bio Hailing from Denver and currently based in the heart of Colorado Springs, Abstraktius Artimus is a versatile force in the independent music scene. A seasoned producer, emcee, sound designer, and audio engineer, his work is defined by an uncompromising commitment to sonic experimentation. With a creative journey spanning nearly three decades, Abstraktius began his trajectory as an emcee in 1997, evolving into a dedicated recording artist by 2002. Over the years, he has built an expansive and diverse body of work, boasting 12 studio albums and a legacy of over 100 high-profile collaborations. Known for pioneering the "Abstrakt Noize" aesthetic, he specializes in weaving foley sound design with precision-engineered beats, resulting in deeply immersive and textured soundscapes. Whether behind the mic or behind the console, Abstraktius Artimus remains a relentless innovator, continuously pushing the boundaries of what experimental hip-hop and electronic production can achieve.
